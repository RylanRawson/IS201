About Me

This is what I do.

I was raised in Okemos, Michigan, which is where I eventually graduated highschool. Following graduation I left home to pursue higher education at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah. I declared my major as Physiology and Developmental Biology and completed two semesters before leaving on a full-time mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Argentina.

Once I got back from my mission I did another two semesters at BYU, but I began exploring different majors than the one I had previously declared. Before too long, I decided that the medical school route was not for me and that I wanted to pursue a degree in business.
